About me

Breaking Things to
Build Better Ones

I'm Kennedy Maingi, a passionate penetration tester and bug bounty hunter based in Nairobi, Kenya. Born on December 12, 2002, I've been obsessed with cybersecurity from a young age — understanding how systems fail is the only way to truly protect them.

My work spans web application security, network penetration testing, API security, mobile security assessments, and vulnerability research on major bug bounty platforms like HackerOne and Bugcrowd.

I believe in responsible disclosure, community education, and pushing the boundaries of what's possible through creativity and technical depth. When I'm not hunting bugs, I'm writing about my findings, contributing to CTF challenges, and mentoring the next generation of African hackers.

Platforms I've found vulnerabilities on include major tech companies, fintech firms, and government portals across Africa and beyond.
